About CarbonClaim

CarbonClaim is a climate technology startup at the ideation stage, with a mission to simplify the process for businesses to access tax credits and financial incentives for adopting sustainable practices. Our focus is on making it easier for companies to leverage benefits associated with carbon credits, renewable energy, and other eco-friendly activities.
